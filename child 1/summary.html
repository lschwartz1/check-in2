Summary of The Garden of Forking Paths by Jorge Luis Borges

This story is about a man named Yu Tsun, who is a German spy in London. However, his cover has been blown and a man named Richard Madden is after him. Now, he must find The Chief, who is apparently his boss, in order to pass on information about the location of British artillery. He is almost caught at the train station by Madden but escapes, and when he gets off he is directed to the man he is looking for on a forking road. He is met by Stephen Albert, who tells him a story about the creator of the garden of forking paths, Ts'ui Pen. Albert reveals the mystery of the garden of forking paths and its legacy. However, Yu Tsen sees Madden approaching, so he shoots Albert in the back. He is sucessfully able to relay the message because the weapons are located in the city of Albert, and they bombed the city.
